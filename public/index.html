Advice

It is not too late.

The mind for this does not exist.

Slow and Steady.

Feature by Feature.

Incremental Progress.

Your brain is a physical part of your body.

Really work on the fundamentals,

but don't stop there.

Be okay with saying "I don't know."

It never hurts to ask.

Always ask for what you need.

Expect to be uncomfortable.

Maintain perspective.

Don't Panic.

Read Error Messages.

Sit with your fear.

Always Be Coding.

Start with why.

End with why.

Check in on why in the middle.
